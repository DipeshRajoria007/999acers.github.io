About 999acers.com

Launched in 2005, 999acres.com, India’s No. 1 property portal, deals with every aspect of the consumers’ needs in the real estate industry. It is an online forum where buyers, sellers and brokers/agents can exchange information about real estate properties quickly, effectively and inexpensively. At 999acres.com, you can advertise a property, search for a property, browse through properties, build your own property microsite, and keep yourself updated with the latest news and trends making headlines in the realty sector.
